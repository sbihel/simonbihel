FTL: Fit The Fittest (2015-2016 Second year student)

Based on the Faster Than Light game, the project's goal was to build the most powerful ships. During the first semester we (5 people including me) made a fighting engine to automatically play combats between ships. Then during the second semester we used genetic algorithms and a bit of data mining to extract the best ships or the best equipment. All that was made in Python.
